About the Questions

These are possible questions you may be asked during your actual UK car driving test.

You'll be asked 2 vehicle safety questions during your car driving test.

  • One 'tell me' question before you start driving
  • One 'show me' question while you are driving

You’ll get one driving fault (sometimes called a ‘minor’) if you get one or both questions wrong.

You’ll fail your driving test if your driving is dangerous or potentially dangerous while you answer the ‘show me’ question.

'Tell Me' Questions

  1. How you’d check that the brakes are working: Brakes should not feel spongy or slack. Test them as you set off. Vehicle should not pull to one side.
  2. Where to find tyre pressure info and how to check: Manufacturer’s guide, use a reliable pressure gauge, check when tyres are cold, include spare, refit valve caps.
  3. Head restraint adjustment: Rigid part at least eye level or top of ears, close to back of head. Some might not be adjustable.
  4. Tyre tread and condition: No cuts/bulges. 1.6mm tread depth across central 3/4 breadth and entire circumference.
  5. Check headlights and tail lights: Operate switch (turn on ignition if needed), walk round vehicle.
  6. Anti-lock braking system warning: Warning light should illuminate if there’s a fault.
  7. Check direction indicators: Operate switch (turn on ignition if needed), walk round vehicle.
  8. Check brake lights: Operate brake pedal, use reflections or ask someone to help.
  9. Power-assisted steering: Gentle pressure on wheel while starting engine or turning wheel after moving off shows if it’s working.
  10. Switch on rear fog lights: Operate switch (with dipped headlights and ignition), check warning light. Explain use.
  11. Switch from dipped to main beam: Operate switch (engine on if needed), check main beam warning light.
  12. Check engine oil: Open bonnet, identify dipstick, describe checking oil level between min/max markers.
  13. Check engine coolant: Open bonnet, identify markings on tank or cap, describe how to top up safely.
  14. Check brake fluid: Open bonnet, identify reservoir, check level between high and low markings.

Note: For questions 12–14, you must open the bonnet and explain the checks.

'Show Me' Questions

  • Show me how you wash and clean the rear windscreen.
  • Show me how you wash and clean the front windscreen.
  • Show me how you’d switch on your dipped headlights.
  • Show me how you’d set the rear demister.
  • Show me how you’d operate the horn.
  • Show me how you’d demist the front windscreen.
  • Show me how you’d open and close the side window.
